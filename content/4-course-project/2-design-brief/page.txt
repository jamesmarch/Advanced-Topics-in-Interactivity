Title: Design Brief

----

Text: Define a problem area in human-computer interaction where you envision making a contribution.<br /> 
—What are the pain points? What is the need for this product to exist?
—How could things be improved? 
—Who is your audience?
—What device(s) can this be deployed on?<br />
Your design brief should contain the following:
—Title page
—Demonstration of problem (images, video)
—Problem Statement
—Problem Description
—Constraints (e.g., time, physical)
—Needs of the Problem (e.g., why should this exist?)
—Goals (e.g., make composting easier)
—What you plan to accomplish (e.g., high-fidelity prototype?)<br />
This brief should be maximum 5 pages.
